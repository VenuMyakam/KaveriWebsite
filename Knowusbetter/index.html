The youngest university in Telangana State, Kaveri University spreads over 100 acres on the outskirts of Hyderabad along the Konda Pochamma Reservoir in Gowraram, Wargal Mandal, Siddipet District. In accordance with the National Education Policy 2020, Kaveri University is a "Multidisciplinary Education and Research University"

Founded in 2023, with the mission to explore the physical world collectively, it aims to expand into 7 Colleges offering more than 40 subjects in diverse streams of study in order to improve the quality of life. Undergraduate, postgraduate, doctorate and post-doctoral programs and courses including Agriculture, Horticulture, Agriculture Engineering & Technology, Food Science &, Technology, Community Science, Basic Sciences, Humanities & Social Sciences, Agribusiness Management and Veterinary programs are offered, with each college supported by 16 specialized "Centres of Excellence, which provide advanced education, training , and research opportunities for students.

Kaveri University's strength lies in its collaboration with Kaveri Seeds research institutes and also in the combined efforts of the various fields of social sciences and emerging Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) technologies. In this way, scientific breakthroughs can be quickly incorporated into education. In order to meet the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), Kaveri University strives to develop and implement pathways of change that address global challenges.

Sustainability is an important pillar of our research, which is driven by the desire to understand and contribute to global challenges. From curiosity-driven fundamental research to science-based and up-scalable practical knowledge, Kaveri combines all knowledge chains to create practical solutions impacting society every day, the Kaveri approach to shaping the new.
