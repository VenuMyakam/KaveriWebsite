The Legacy of Shaping a Green revolution

One of India’s fastest growing and largest agriculture companies, Kaveri Seeds specializes in hybrid seeds for Indian food and industrial crops. Sri G.V. Bhaskar Rao, in 1976, started the company with the objective of fuelling India’s Green Revolution through his start-up G.V.B. Rao & Company. Subsequently, it became Kaveri Seed Company Private Limited in 1986.

Kaveri Group includes two subsidiaries – Aditya Agritech Private Limited and Kaveri Microteck Private Limited. Aditya Agritech Private Limited is engaged in marketing and distribution of Hybrid Seeds and Kaveri Microteck specializes in developing and producing a wide array of organic/ biofertilizer/micronutrient mixtures/ biopesticide input products both for soil application and foliar spray.

Kaveri's high yielding seeds in diverse crops are backed by strong Research & Development, making the company farmers’ trusted partner. Kaveri has developed research collaborations with several national and international institutions to strengthen in-house research efforts. For over half a decade, Kaveri has continually improved its products & processes to enhance farm productivity while ensuring food and nutritional security.
